ZopCloud · private preview · early 2026

Multi-cloud, drawn.

A live resource graph across AWS, GCP, and Azure. Every dependency, every drift, every blast radius — on one map. Currently in private preview with a small group of design partners.

live · 14 nodes · 23 edges
what it sees

Three views of the same truth.

Built on the same inventory ZopNight uses. The graph isn't a re-import — it's just turning the data sideways.

Resource graph

Every cloud resource as a node, every dependency as an edge. Filter by environment, owner, region, cost. Click a node to see what depends on it — and what it depends on.

Topology view

The architecture diagram you wish you'd kept up to date. Auto-laid-out from the graph, exports as PNG / SVG / Mermaid. Updates the second something changes.

Drift detection

Terraform says one thing, the cloud says another. ZopCloud notices the moment they disagree, names the resource, the field, and who changed it. No more 2am surprises.

roadmap

Where we are. Where we're going.

Public roadmap so design partners know what's next. Dates are best-guesses, not promises.

Q1 2026 · now

Resource graph & topology

AWS + GCP support. Live graph, auto-layout, exports. Limited to 10 design-partner orgs.

Q2 2026 · next

Drift detection & alerts

Terraform & Pulumi support. Slack & webhook alerts. Per-team drift budgets.

Q3 2026

Azure & Kubernetes

Full parity with AWS / GCP. K8s as a sub-graph with namespace and workload nodes.

Q4 2026 · GA

Public GA

Self-serve onboarding. Full API. Plugged into ZopNight scheduling and ZopDay deploys.
