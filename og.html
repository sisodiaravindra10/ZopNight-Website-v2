§ 16 · open graph + social meta

Link previews, on brand.

Every page on zop.dev gets a 1200×630 SVG served as og:image. Five templates cover every surface type. Fill in the headline, the metric, or the title; the layout does the rest. LinkedIn, X, Slack, Teams previews all consume these.

§ 01 · default · homepage + fallback

The three-numbers meta shot.

Homepage, about, any page without a specific variant. Dark canvas with the full lockup top-left and the three-numbers strip along the bottom. Answers “what is ZopDev?” in one image.

§ 06 · integration

Wiring meta tags.

Drop these in every page’s <head>. Prefer SVG where platforms support it; fall back to rasterized 1200×630 PNG at og/og-default.png (not included here — export via any SVG-to-PNG tool or the rsvg-convert CLI).

<!-- Open Graph -->
<meta property="og:type" content="website"/>
<meta property="og:title" content="ZopDev · the Continuous CloudOps platform"/>
<meta property="og:description" content="See everything. Find what's wrong. Fix it in one click."/>
<meta property="og:url" content="https://zop.dev/"/>
<meta property="og:image" content="https://zop.dev/og/og-default.png"/>
<meta property="og:image:width" content="1200"/>
<meta property="og:image:height" content="630"/>

<!-- Twitter / X -->
<meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image"/>
<meta name="twitter:site" content="@zopdev"/>

<meta name="twitter:description" content="See everything. Find what's wrong. Fix it in one click."/>

§ 07 · platform behaviour

Where each platform crops.

Same 1200×630 source, three different crops in the wild. Keep all critical content (logo, headline, URL anchor) inside the safe zone — the central 1024×512 area.

01 · LinkedIn feed

Centered crop · title below.

Keeps: full 1200×630 image in feed. Title and description render underneath.

02 · X / Twitter

2:1 crop · 1200×600.

Crops: ~15px off top and bottom. Safe zone centered. URL + title render below.

03 · Slack · Teams

Inline preview card.

Renders: full image above title + description + URL. No cropping but smaller size.

§ 08 · rules

Do · Don’t.

DO
  • Keep critical content inside the central 1024×512 safe zone
  • Match the URL anchor text to the actual URL
  • Export SVG source; render PNG fallback at 1200×630
  • Use the same three-stripe pillar bar at the top of every template
  • Keep one pillar color dominant per variant — blue, orange, or green
  • Always include the three-tile mark somewhere (top-left or bottom-right)
DON'T
  • No screenshots of the product UI inside OG images
  • No stock photography · no gradients · no shadows with blur
  • No light-canvas variant (the brand is dark on shared links)
  • No more than 20 words in the headline
  • No hashtag clusters or emoji in the preview image
  • No animation — platforms render static only
