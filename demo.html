03 · faq

Common questions.

How long is the demo?

15 minutes is the minimum, we show you the Atlas inventory, run the findings panel, and apply one remediation live. If you want to go deeper on a specific area (Kubernetes cost, multi-account, Terraform integration), we can go to 30–45 minutes. We follow your lead.

Do I need to connect my cloud account?

No. We use a synthetic $200K/month AWS account for all demos, it has real findings, real recommendations, and a realistic cost distribution. If you want to connect your own account during the demo, we can do that too. It takes about 5 minutes and uses read-only IAM permissions, we can see everything but change nothing without your approval.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. After the demo, you can start a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. The trial includes full ZopNight functionality with up to 3 cloud accounts and all remediation features. If you need more time or accounts, just ask and we'll extend it.
