company · about

The team building the cloud autopilot.

ZopDev is the unified platform for managing AWS, Azure, OCI, and GCP from a single interface. Security, compliance, observability, and automation come built-in — engineers focus on shipping product, not wrangling infrastructure. We're a small group, distributed across four markets, building in the open and shipping every week.

01 · mission

The cloud promised efficiency. Elasticity. Pay-for-what-you-use. What most teams got instead was sprawl, thousands of resources provisioned over years by dozens of engineers, no single person understanding the whole picture, and a bill that grew 30% year-over-year no matter what anyone did.

FinOps tried to help. But FinOps as it's typically practiced is a reporting discipline, not an engineering one. Dashboards that show you what you spent. Reports that explain why. Quarterly reviews where the same waste shows up again.

ZopDev is building the continuous CloudOps platform that makes cloud optimization automatic, not a project. Connect your accounts. Let the CDCR loop run. Watch the waste disappear, permanently, not just until next quarter.

02 · founding story

Why we started.

ZopDev was founded in 2024 by a team that had spent years inside large engineering organizations, fixing cloud messes for Fortune 500 companies, watching teams struggle with DevOps bottlenecks and scaling issues that slowed innovation.

The pattern was always the same. A new project meant weeks of YAML, scripts, security reviews, and observability stitching before a single line of product code shipped. Multi-cloud meant the same problem multiplied by every provider in the stack. The compliance review at the end almost always sent the team back to start.

"We built ZopDev as the shortcut we wished we had, where security, compliance, observability, and automation come built-in. Now developers can focus on building products instead of wrangling infrastructure." – Talvinder Singh, Co-founder & CEO

We started in markets where engineering teams move fastest, the USA, Singapore, and the UK, and have since expanded into India, one of the fastest-growing startup ecosystems globally. The mission stays the same everywhere: production-ready, fully compliant cloud infrastructure in under 30 minutes, with zero manual scripts.

04 · values

How we work.

These aren't aspirational, they're operational. You'll see them in how the product works, how we communicate, and what we build next.

Continuous vs Quarterly

Cloud waste compounds weekly. A quarterly review catches it after it's already cost you money. We build for continuous detection and remediation, the loop runs every time something changes, not every 90 days.

Automated vs Manual

If a remediation is safe and repeatable, it should happen automatically, with your approval, not after a meeting. We build automation that respects the human in the loop while eliminating the human from the loop for work that doesn't need one.

Transparent vs Opaque

Every finding tells you exactly why it was flagged. Every recommendation shows its utilization data. Every remediation is logged. We don't make black-box recommendations, we show the math, always.

Engineering-first vs Sales-first

Our demo is a 15-minute live walkthrough of the product, not a slide deck. Our changelog is public. Our roadmap is public. We answer technical questions before we talk about pricing. Engineers trust engineers.
